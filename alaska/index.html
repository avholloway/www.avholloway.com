Pacific Northwest & Alaska Adventure

June 2026 • Seattle • Olympic National Park • Cruise

Exploring Seattle

Our journey began in the Emerald City. We spent our time exploring the iconic waterfront, wandering through the bustling energy of Pike Place Market, and catching some incredible views of the city skyline before heading out into the wilderness.

Olympic National Park

A short side trip brought us to the breathtaking landscapes of the Olympic Peninsula. From the moss-draped, deep greens of the Hoh Rain Forest to the rugged, driftwood-lined Pacific coastline, the sheer diversity of the wilderness was unforgettable.

Sailing the Inside Passage

Then, we set sail for Alaska. Navigating through the massive, glassy fjords, watching giant chunks of tidewater glaciers calve into the sea, and spotting local wildlife right from the deck made for an incredible experience on the water.
