Summer Break Family Cruise

June 2026 • Holland American Lines

Setting Sail

Getting everyone boarded and settled in our staterooms. The weather was perfect as we pulled away from the port, and we spent the first afternoon exploring the upper decks and getting our bearings on the ship.

Specialty Dining

One of the absolute highlights was the specialty dining. It was a great opportunity to sit down as a family away from the main buffet crowds, enjoy some incredible food, and recap our favorite moments from the day.

Shore Excursions

Our pre-booked excursions were totally worth it. Getting off the ship to explore the local ports gave us a break from sea days and let us stretch our legs while taking in the local sights and culture.
